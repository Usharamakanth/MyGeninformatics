Privacy Policy

At MyGen Informatics, we take data protection seriously and follow appropriate measures to prevent data misuse and unauthorized access. When completing the ‘contact us’ form on our website your personal details such as name and Email address are collected in order to reach you back. We use this data only to contact you back and is never used for any other marketing purposes or never shared with third-parties. We store/process the data as per General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) guidelines. You have the right to delete and withdraw your consent to store your personal data with us. You also have the right to request the data we stored, and you will be sent the information we collected from you through our website. Please send your requests/queries regarding the personal data and privacy policy to info@mygeninformatics.nl.

All the research data that we receive from our clients will be stored, processed and analyzed on our own servers locally. If we use any secured third-party services such as AWS or GoogleCloud to process, analyze, store or transfer raw or analyzed research data, this will be informed properly to the client. Any data, either personal or research data is never shared with any other third-parties that are not associated with MyGen Informatics. If you have any queries about data policy, please reach us at info@mygeninformatics.nl.
