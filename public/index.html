ABOUT

Our vision is to harness the power of bioinformatics and data analysis to empower our clients to make data-driven research with confidence. We believe that the overwhelming amount of biological data generated in today’s rapidly evolving life sciences sector, holds immense potential for revolutionary breakthroughs. However, extracting meaningful insights from this vast information requires not only expertise and advanced computational methods but also a deep understanding of complex biological systems. That's where we come in. By combining cutting-edge technologies with our unwavering commitment to innovation and excellence, we strive to overcome complex challenges in understanding the biological data to drive transformative discoveries and beyond.

Our values form the foundation of our company, guiding our decisions and actions. We nurture a culture of collaboration, working hand-in-hand with our clients to cultivate strong relationships built on trust and open communication. We are dedicated to delivering excellence in all aspects of our work, constantly striving for innovation, accuracy, and the highest quality outcomes to make a meaningful impact with bioinformatics.

SERVICES

Data Analysis

Do you want to get the most out of your raw sequencing data? Be it from genomes or transcriptomes or ChIP-seq or any other sequencing either bulk or single-cell data? We help you with all sorts of data analysis.

Data Integration

Do you want to integrate multiple 'omics' datasets and compare it across multiple levels, diverse domains & kingdoms of life? Then you are in the right place.

Evolution

Do you also believe that “Nothing in Biology Makes Sense Except in the Light of Evolution”? Reach us and we make the gene, SNP or species phylogenies for you that make sense, with our long-standing expertise in this area.

Data Management

We help you manage your data, create pipelines, databases, websites, visualization tools and toolkits to explore data quickly and efficiently. We also provide direct support to your lab/team members and scientific staff to use the tools and databases.

Data Curation

Bioinformatics has to deal with curating and using open-source data. Do you have a biological question that can be answered using public biological data repositories, but unsure how to deal with it? We help you retrieve, analyze and interpret open-source data to answer your questions.

Consultancy and Beyond

We provide contract bioinformatics support and consultancy services for data-driven life science research. Or if you are interested in working together on a long-term project which needs more strategic planning and flexibility, then please get in touch. We are very happy to help you, or to get you in touch with experts who can help elsewhere making an effective collaboration, in close working relationship with you.

Our Bioinformatics Capabilities

Next-generation as well as Third-generation (i.e. short and long-read) sequencing data analysis such as from Illumina, MGI, Nanopore and PacBio

DNA-seq, RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, DAP-seq and any other sequencing techniques

Gene family, SNP-based and species phylogenies

Reference-based as well as de novo assemblies for genomes and transcriptomes

Gene prediction and genome annotation

Polymorphism (SNP/InDel) identification, impact assessment and structural variation (SV) detection

Data (networks) visualization and exploration using R, Cytoscape and Python

Protein modeling, structure comparison and molecular dynamics simulations

Data integration and database management – SQL and MongoDB
